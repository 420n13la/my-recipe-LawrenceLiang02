Name: Fried Rice

Description:

The fried rice is a known dish in the chinese culture. It is a mixture of rice, vegetables and meat. The content may vary.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Sesame oil
  • Peas and Carror
  • Eggs
  • Shrimp

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Start by cooking the meat.
  3. When the meat is cooked, add the eggs and the vegetables.
  4. Once all the top steps are done, put all of it together and add some soya sauce.
